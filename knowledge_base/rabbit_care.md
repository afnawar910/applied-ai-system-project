# Rabbit Care Guidance

## Feeding

Rabbits need reliable feeding routines, hay access, and fresh water. A sudden change in appetite can be urgent and should be taken seriously.

## Enrichment

Rabbits benefit from safe chew toys, hideouts, tunnels, and supervised exercise space. Enrichment should avoid unsafe materials or small parts that can be swallowed.

## Grooming

Gentle brushing helps with shedding. Rabbits can be sensitive to handling, so grooming should be calm and low-stress.
