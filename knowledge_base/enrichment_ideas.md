# Enrichment Ideas

## Low-Time Enrichment

When the owner has limited time, useful enrichment can still be short: puzzle feeders, scent searches, training cues, gentle brushing, or a five-minute play session.

## Priority Balance

Enrichment supports mental health, but it usually comes after feeding, medication, and urgent health needs when the schedule is over budget.

## Notes and Preferences

Task notes can capture what the pet enjoys or dislikes. The planner should use notes to suggest realistic activities rather than generic care ideas.
