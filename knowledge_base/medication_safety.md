# Medication Safety

## Medication Priority

Medication tasks should be treated as high-priority schedule items when they are prescribed or recommended by a veterinarian. If time is limited, medication should not be replaced by lower-priority activities.

## Safety Guardrails

Follow the medication label and veterinarian instructions. Do not change dosage, stop medication, or add new medication based only on a planning app.

## Missed Medication

If a medication dose is missed or timing is unclear, contact a veterinarian or follow the written instructions provided with the medication.
