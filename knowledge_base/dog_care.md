# Dog Care Guidance

## Exercise and Walks

Most dogs benefit from consistent walks because exercise supports behavior, digestion, and energy management. Shorter daily walks are often more useful than rare long sessions, especially when the owner's time budget is limited.

## Feeding Routines

Dogs do best with predictable feeding routines. Feeding should be prioritized when the schedule is tight because skipped meals can disrupt medication timing, appetite tracking, and daily structure.

## Enrichment

Enrichment can include fetch, scent games, puzzle feeders, and short training sessions. When time is limited, choose brief enrichment that fits around high-priority health tasks.
