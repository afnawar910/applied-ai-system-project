# Cat Care Guidance

## Feeding and Hydration

Cats rely on consistent feeding routines and access to fresh water. Missed meals can be important to notice because appetite changes may signal stress or illness.

## Play and Enrichment

Short play sessions with wand toys, laser toys, or food puzzles can support exercise and reduce boredom. Cats often respond well to several brief sessions instead of one long activity.

## Grooming

Brushing can reduce shedding and hairballs, especially for long-haired cats. Grooming should stay calm and brief if the cat becomes stressed.
